Ingredients:
 - 1 hot dog, preferably one with a minimum of chicken beaks and rat droppings
 - 1 hot dog bun
 - 1 pot of water
 - 1 packet of ketchup (stop here if are you from the south)
 - 1 packet of mustard, some onions, and relish (for everybody else)
 - optional hot peppers

Directions:
1) Boil water
2) Put hotdog in
3) After all pathogens are eliminated, place hot dog in bun
4) Add fixings
5) Take a bit out of hot dog
6) Breathe in and out quickly to account for all the accumulated heat in hot dog
